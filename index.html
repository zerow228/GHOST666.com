Ghost666(Sixer)

Guest 666 is a killer in Forsaken. A Guest Robloxian who was twisted and morphed into a devilish creature barely recognizable from who they were.
Using their large size, Guest 666 pressures and crushes anyone across their path like a twisted animal.

Appearance

Guest 666's large body is similar to the body type of Panthera mammals with elements of Devils.
Their body is fully black with several details being colored in red, such as; a tail — having the Roblox logo at the end, their hands having large claws with cracks at the joints, thick red fur around their neck, and large red horns.
Guest 666 is also wearing old clothing based on the outfit of the classic Guest avatar, which consists of a black hoodie with the Roblox logo on the front, a red baseball hat, and black pants.

Strategy

Guest 666 is a very gimmicky killer, requiring specific strategies to deal with survivors. Guest 666 dominates in open maps with insane speed, but they struggle against twists and tight corners. Guest 666 players should aim to strategize and prioritize saving up Blood Orbs outside of their Blood Hunt, rather than aiming to kill. The main issue that Guest 666 will encounter is loops, which has a few solutions such as activating your Manic Fixation or using Demonic Pursuit to jump on top of the loop, scaring them off and putting them in a situation that directly benefits you- a straight, uninterrupted chase. To master this character, it requires more thinking than most other killers, while also making sure to balance the round's clock as well as your Blood Orbs to kill the survivors in one fatal strike.
